## üîó How to Subscribe

### iCloud Calendar (iOS/macOS)
1. Copy the subscription URL from above
2. Open Calendar app
3. Go to File ‚Üí New Calendar Subscription (macOS) or Settings ‚Üí Accounts ‚Üí Add Account ‚Üí Other ‚Üí Add CalDAV Account (iOS)
4. Paste the URL and click Subscribe
5. Choose refresh frequency (recommended: Daily)

### Google Calendar
1. Open Google Calendar
2. Click the "+" next to "Other calendars"
3. Select "From URL"
4. Paste the subscription URL
5. Click "Add calendar"

### Outlook
1. Open Outlook
2. Go to Calendar
3. Click "Add calendar" ‚Üí "Subscribe from web"
4. Paste the subscription URL
5. Click "Import"
